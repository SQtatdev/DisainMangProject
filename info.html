🃏 Blackjack (21) 🖤♠️

🎯 Goal

Beat the 🧑‍⚖️ dealer without going over 21.

🂡 Card Values

  • 2–10 = face value 🔢
  • J, Q, K = 10 👑
  • Ace = 1 or 11 🎭

🃞 The Deal

  • You get 2 cards
  • Dealer shows 1, hides 1 🕵️
  • Blackjack = A + 10 🖤

✋ Your Options

  • Hit ➕🃏 – Take another card
  • Stand ✋ – Keep your hand
  • Double Down 💰 – Double bet, one more card
  • Split ✂️ – Two same cards = two hands
  • Surrender 🏳️ – Forfeit half, end early (if allowed)

🤖 Dealer Rules

  • Hits until 17 or higher 🔄
  • Must follow table rules 📜

🏆 Win If:

  • You're closer to 21 than dealer
  • Dealer busts 💥
  • You get Blackjack 💣 and dealer doesn’t

💀 Lose If:

  • You bust
  • Dealer is closer

🤝 Push:

Tie = no win, no loss
